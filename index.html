The Smart-H2O

IoT-based water quality monitoring system is a cost-effective practice of using IoT technology to monitor and manage water quality in water bodies. It gathers data on water bodies using sensors, meters and other devices and leverages IoT gateway and platform to integrate, analyze, and process the data, in order to implement remote monitoring and early warning of water quality. IoT water quality monitoring system is suited for monitoring residential and industrial drainage, as well as natural water pollution. It can track a wide range of water quality indicators, including temperature, pH value, dissolved oxygen, turbidity, conductivity, and others.
